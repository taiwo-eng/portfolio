Parpend

Parpend is an independently owned design studio based in Lagos, Nigeria whose work encompasses design across different fields.

About

Design Studio

Parpend works with businesses to communicate and connect with people using user-centered design methods, ensuring they have profound impact on their commnunities.

In 2018, we set up Parpend design because we were fed up with how design was being approached and executed by advertising firms in Nigeria. The less-than-appropriate approach to design has led to a terrible misconception of what design is and the value it brings to businesses locally.

We started Parpend to do design work we couldn't do in a regular 9 to 5. It gives us the opportunity to explore design for environmental applications, emerging media, and social causes.

Partner to startups

Because of our unique combination of experience and skills, we are able to quickly embed ourselves in scenarios and solve design problems for startups and non-profits. We partner with founders and startups at their different stages to work on projects, build and grow their design culture internally, while they focus on building profitable businesses.
